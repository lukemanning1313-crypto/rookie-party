Phase 1 — The Arrival

12:00 – 12:20 PM
Setup
The Walk-In
Every rookie gets handed a beer at the door. You drink it before you sit down. No exceptions. Music on, energy building.
Reveal
The Draft
Bukes & Reggie announce their teams. Each rookie joins their captain. Teams get 2 minutes to pick a team name and war cry.
★ The war cry comes back later — make it count

Phase 2 — Warm-Up Rounds

12:20 – 12:50 PM
Round 1
Flip Cup Gauntlet
Classic flip cup, team vs team. Best of 3. Start normal, then blindfolded, then non-dominant hand.
Losing team: everyone takes a penalty shot
Round 2
Beer Pong Blitz
Speed pong — 5 min time limit. Whoever sinks more cups wins. Both teams play simultaneously or alternate shots.
Losing team: finish all remaining cups
Round 3
Rookie Trivia
Rapid-fire questions about the team, the league, vets' stats, Grizzlies history. One rookie from each team goes head-to-head. Rotate each question.
Wrong answer = drink. Hesitation = double.
★ Vets supply the questions

Phase 3 — The Gauntlet

12:50 – 1:40 PM • Where legends are made
Gauntlet Round 1
Shotgun Relay
Teams line up single file. Relay-style shotgun race — one finishes, next one starts. First team through all members wins.
Losing team: every member takes another penalty shot
Gauntlet Round 2
The Hot Seat
One rookie sits in front of everyone. Vets fire rapid questions — embarrassing stories, "who on this team would...", hockey trivia. 30 seconds on the clock.
Every wrong answer or hesitation = drink
★ Team with fewest total drinks wins the round
Gauntlet Round 3
Captain's Pick
Each captain picks one rookie from THE OTHER team. Those two go head-to-head in a chugging race. Pure 1v1.
Loser's ENTIRE team drinks
★ Strategy matters — pick wisely
Gauntlet Round 4
The Coliseum (Dizzy Bat)
Each captain picks one rookie from THEIR OWN team. Both go down to the parking lot, one at a time — everyone else watches from the balcony. 10 spins on the bat, then a vet pitches from ~15 feet.
Round 1: Softball — big, slow, hittable. Just getting their bearings.
Round 2: Tennis ball — smaller, faster. This is where it gets ugly.
Round 3: Ping pong ball — basically impossible. Pure comedy.
Hit = point for your team. Miss = that rookie drinks.
★ Tied after 3? Sudden death — both spin again, first contact wins
Gauntlet Round 5
War Cry from The Balcony
Both teams go to the balcony. Each team performs their war cry from The Draft to the parking lot below. Vets judge volume, energy, creativity.
Losing team: shotgun on the balcony
★ The spectacle moment of the day
Gauntlet Round 6
Last Man Standing
Musical chairs meets drinking. Music plays, chairs get pulled. When you're out, you take a shot. Last rookie standing wins a golden advantage.
Each elimination = a shot
★ Winner earns 50 BONUS POINTS for scavenger hunt

Phase 4 — The Send-Off

1:40 – 2:00 PM
Final
The Bus Chug
Full team chug race — every rookie chugs simultaneously. Winning team picks seats on the bus first.
Ceremony
Balcony Send-Off
Group photo on the balcony. Captains give a quick speech. Announce the apartment winner and the scavenger hunt bonus. Head to the bus.
★ Bus leaves at 2:00 SHARP
