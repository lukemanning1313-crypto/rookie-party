Vets Run The Show
Punishment stacks round to round. Losing team carries penalties forward. Don't be the weak link.

Intake

12:00 – 12:20 PM • Set the tone immediately
Order 01
The Door Tax
Every rookie gets handed a beer the second they walk in. You chug it standing in the doorway before you're allowed to enter. Vets watch. No talking.
Slowest chugger: does it again
Order 02
The Lineup
All rookies line up against the wall. One at a time, step forward — state your name, where you're from, and one thing about yourself. Vets heckle freely. This is not a safe space.
Weak intro = you drink and try again
Order 03
Squad Assignment
Bukes & Reggie step up and claim their rookies one by one. You don't pick your team — your team picks you. Each squad gets 2 minutes to come up with a war cry. You'll need it.
★ The war cry comes back later. Put effort in or pay for it.

Basic Training

12:20 – 12:50 PM • Start earning your spot
Drill 01
Flip Cup — Escalating
Team vs team. Best of 3. Round 1: normal. Round 2: non-dominant hand. Round 3: blindfolded. Losing team stays at the table.
Losing team: penalty shot + 20 pushups. Each member.
★ First loss of the day carries forward into The Gauntlet
Drill 02
Shotgun Drill
Vets yell "SHOTGUN" at random. Every rookie grabs a beer and goes. Last rookie to finish each time gets marked. 3 marks = you're in The Hot Seat later.
3 marks = mandatory Hot Seat selection
★ Stay alert. This can happen at ANY point during Basic Training.
Drill 03
Vet Knowledge Check
Vets walk down the line asking each rookie a question — jersey numbers, hometowns, positions, stats. You should've done your homework.
Wrong answer = chug. Hesitation = chug + wall sit until the next wrong answer.

The Gauntlet

12:50 – 1:45 PM • Punishment stacks. Don't be last.
Stacking Rule
The team that lost Basic Training starts The Gauntlet with a penalty — every member does a shotgun before Round 1 begins. From here, every loss adds to the next round.
Round 1
The Hot Seat
Rookies get called up one at a time. Vets fire questions: "Roast yourself." "Sing right now." "Most embarrassing moment — go." "Who's the worst player on your team?" 30 seconds on the clock per rookie. No hesitation.
Hesitation or weak answer = drink + 10 pushups on the spot
★ Vets vote on who had the weakest seat — that rookie drinks double
Round 2
Shotgun Relay
Teams line up. Relay-style shotgun race — one finishes, next starts. But the losing team from Basic Training starts 1 beer behind (stacking penalty).
Losing team: wall sit holding a full beer. Drop it = start over.
Round 3
Captain's Sacrifice
Each captain points at one rookie from THE OTHER team. Those two go 1v1 — chug race. But here's the twist: loser doesn't just drink. Loser's entire team does 15 burpees while the winner's team watches.
Losing rookie: extra penalty shot on top of the burpees
★ Captains — choose the guy you think will crack
Round 4
The Coliseum
Everyone to the balcony. Each captain sends one rookie down to the parking lot. 10 spins on the bat, then a vet pitches from 15 feet. Entire balcony watches and heckles.
Pitch 1: Softball — big target. Getting their bearings.
Pitch 2: Tennis ball — this is where it gets ugly.
Pitch 3: Ping pong ball — basically impossible. Pure comedy.
Every miss = that rookie chugs in the parking lot while the balcony counts
★ Tied after 3? Sudden death — both spin again, first contact wins
Round 5
The Balcony
While everyone's still outside — both teams perform their war cry to the parking lot below. Full volume. Full commitment. Vets judge effort, not creativity. Half-ass it and you'll know.
Losing team: shotgun on the balcony while the other team heckles
Round 6
Last Man Standing
Musical chairs with consequences. Every elimination: that rookie takes a shot. Between each round, remaining rookies hold a wall sit. Fall off the wall = eliminated. Last one standing earns something.
Each elimination = shot + you're done for the day (you watch)
★ Last rookie standing earns 50 bonus scavenger hunt points for their team

The Send-Off

1:45 – 2:00 PM • Earn your seat
Final Order
The Bus Chug
Every rookie chugs simultaneously. Full team race. Winning team boards the bus first and picks seats. Losing team stands outside and waits until vets say they can board.
Send-Off
Balcony Photo & March Out
Group photo on the balcony. Captains give a 30-second speech. Announce who's carrying the most penalties into the scavenger hunt. Everyone heads to the bus.
★ Bus leaves at 2:00 SHARP. Not on the bus = you're walking downtown.
